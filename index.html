Research

Foucusing on fundamental mathematical physics


Laminar-turbulent Transition

Exploring the underlying physical mechanism (in classic flow types, e.g. pipe flow, PPF, and PCF) with dynamic system approach, brute-force DNS, experimental data, and machine learning tools.
Establishing fist-principles theoretical model to provide profound comprehension and predictive insights.


Forecast for Complex Systems

Utilizing physical-informed machine learning tools to forecast behaviors of complex systems, and interpreting forecast performance by dynamic system approac and DNS.
